Why is Zakat important?

Zakat is vital because it benefits not only the individual but also the recipient and society at large. The power of Zakat cannot be underestimated. It's an incredible institution intended to transform society.


Imagine this: if just the top ten wealthiest people in the world paid Zakat, that would amount to £7.7 billion! That would lift countless people from poverty. The effects on the world would be huge if we all just gave our part.
